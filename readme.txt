6 cats to choose from:
	Each cat has different HP, ATK and Type.


Each player get to choose 2 cats (don't have to be unique).  Each cat has 3 moves:
	1. A basic attack.  Attacks hit for a random amount within a certain range.

	2. A special attack.  Special attacks have a type, and some specials types are more/less effective against certain cat types.

	3. A buff/debuff.


Player 1 has the option to pick last, or go first.


Each turn a player has the option to use 1 of the 3 moves, or they may choose to swap which one of their cats is fighting.


A player wins when they kill both of the other player's cats.
