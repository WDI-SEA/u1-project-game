CAT FIGHT is a Pokémon-style battle game.  Each player will pick 2 cats and battle them to the death!  Each cat has a Special which can be better/worse against certain types, so pick carefully!  You'll choose your cats in a snake draft (A-B-B-A) on the next page, so if you choose to pick last, you will also pick first.
